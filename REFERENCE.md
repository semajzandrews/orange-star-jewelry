## The bar (synthesis)
Award-grade atelier sites treat the site as a PLACE and a STORY, not a catalog.
They open on atmosphere (a workshop, a material, the maker's hand) before any
price, hold a disciplined 2 to 3 color palette with one warm metallic accent,
use confident editorial display type as art direction, and move with slow
scroll-coupled motion. They foreground material honesty and the maker: "made by
this person, for you." Discount-jeweler sites lead with inventory, sale badges,
and stock-grid clip-art. Ours leads with the bench, the torch, and Rahim's hands.
